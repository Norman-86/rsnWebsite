Life at Research Solutions Network

Working at Research Solutions Network is a great rewarding experience. We have a great team composed of local and international experts. We offer an awesome flexible work environment. We have very flexible working hours, competitive salaries with regular performance based salary and career development reviews. Our team receives occassional technical and professional trainings as we focus on employee development.

Technical Research & Evaluation Manager

Lead Research and Evaluation will supervise project managers in Research design and implementation; coordinate with program implementers as well as principal investigators so as to ensure implementation feasibility and also fidelity to research protocols. The candidate will be responsible to write, prepare and present research findings and projects at various public forums including academic and non academic audience as well as coordinating the internal dissemination of the findings.

Requirements

The candidate should have a PhD or Master Degree in Research and Evaluation, Monitoring, Economics or Statistics and any other relevant discipline with at least 3 years working experience in the field of research and evaluation, extensive knowledge in methodology, data analysis SPSS, STATA, R, Epi-Info, and digital data mining and management. Should possess sufficient knowledge in the field of research and evaluation. Salient and commendable managerial, administrative, analytical and decision making skills. Ability to analyze, understand and discuss new programme design and implementation approaches.

Responsibilities

  • To supervise project managers in Research design and implementation, coordinate with program implementers as well as principal investigators so as to ensure implementation feasibility and also fidelity to research protocols.
  • Develop capacity of the research team through one to one mentoring, workshop and external resources.
  • Promote inter project based practices and take the lead in sharing these practices with project through formal and informal trainings.
  • Write proposals and prepare budget for the new projects, manage and allocate financial resources of all projects in the unit.
  • Liaise with other research institution, NGOs, Government agencies, independent researches, academia, funders and others with an interest of fostering collaboration and learning.
  • Write, prepare and present research findings and projects at various public forums including academic and non academic audience as well as coordinating the internal dissemination of the findings.

Research Analyst

Research Solutions Network is seeking an application for the post of Research Analyst. The Research Analyst develops and maintain, as advised by the Lead Researcher, close research collaboration with academics, and researchers in various organizations. The candidate will be responsible to provide substantive support to the ideas of the Lead Researcher on formulating the theme and the structure of the brainstorming meetings and in-house discussions on various aspects of the Report development. The candidate must have a solid academic record, with a PhD or Practical Master’s degree with a focus on social, economic, or technology aspects of development with minimum 2 years of relevant work experience, preferably in areas of research.

Requirements

  • The candidate must have a solid academic record, with a PhD or Master’s degree with a strong affiliation to social, economic, or technology research.
  • Practical and demonstrated quantitative, qualitative and blend analytical expertise using STATA, SPSS, R, NNIVO and MATLAB. He/she should be at peace with Mathematics!
  • Minimum 2 years of relevant work experience, preferably in areas of research
  • Have a good grasp of substantive and policy issues related to the study of social and economic development, and of the human development and capabilities approach
  • Very good writing skills and ability to produce memos and notes with very short notice.
  • Excellent command of English (oral and written).
