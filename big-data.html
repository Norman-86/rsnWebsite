Big Data- The Possibilities

by Admin | Category big data | May 4th, 2021

0 Comments

What It Means

Big Data is here; Big Data is emminent.Big data is a technology term used to define the exponential growth and availability of structured, semi-structured and unstructured information that is requisite for decision-making. In the last decade, effective use of big data has led to more accurate analyses and helped organizations to leverage on operational efficiencies, risk and cost reductions.

Significance of Big Data

Research Solutions Network studied Big Data in seven sectors of the Kenyan economy â€“ telecommunications, education, public sector, healthcare, retail, agriculture and manufacturing. The study revealed for instance that a business in the retail industry using big data effectively could increase its operating margins by more than 62 percent. Effective application of big data in the public sector has enormous potential of reducing losses, fraud and errors by 84%, implying the government can save huge amounts of money every year and increased efficiency. If Kenyan healthcare was to use big data creatively and effectively to drive efficiency and quality, the sector could reduce its expenditure by 29%.


Big data can generate value and insight in every sector of the economy:
1. Big data makes information transparent and usable at much higher frequency thereby enabling businesses to make decisions and implement them faster.
2. It helps organizations come up with accurate and detailed performance information on every segment of the business and therefore expose variability and boost performance.
3. Companies can also use big data to conduct controlled experiments to make better management decisions, business forecasting and adjustment of business levers in real-time. This can also help in creating robust business models that allow organizations to stay ahead of competition.
4. Big data can be used to inform the development new products and services. It can allow businesses to create innovative after-sales service offerings, proactive maintenance and tailor-made products for the next generations.
5. Effective use of big data has become a key basis of competition and growth for many organizations. Established firms and new entrants into the market are leveraging data-driven strategies to innovate and compete.

The State of Big Data in Kenyan SMEs

More than 70% of SMEs in Kenya have online presence. This has exposed them to the greater potential of using big data to scale their sales, competition, design new business models and drive innovation. However, less than 20% of these businesses have taken advantage of big data to spur productivity and growth because most of the social and infrastructure based data is left unstructured and unanalyzed.

The increasing volumes and details of information captured by institutions/organizations, the rise of multimedia, social media and the Internet of Things has fostered exponential growth in data and every business leader/manager should have a strategy of using Big Data to create change in their organizations. Research Solutions Network has experienced big data professionals and tool that can help you take full advantage of your big data and create value for your organization.
